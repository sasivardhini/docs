## What is DevEntry Job Tracker?

DevEntry Job Tracker is an **AI-powered API** that automatically tracks your job applications by analyzing your Gmail inbox. Using **Gemini 2.5 Flash** AI, it intelligently classifies job-related emails and organizes them into a comprehensive job board.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Your Gmail">
    Authenticate via Google OAuth to grant read-only access to your Gmail account
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create an API Key">
    Generate a secure API key linked to your Gmail account and choose a pricing tier
  </Step>
  <Step title="Trigger a Sync">
    Call the `/v2/sync` endpoint to fetch and analyze emails from the last 24 hours
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get Your Job Board">
    Retrieve organized job applications via `/v2/jobs` grouped by application status
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Technology Stack

- **Backend**: FastAPI (Python)
- **AI Model**: Google Gemini 2.5 Flash
- **Authentication**: Google OAuth 2.0
- **Database**: MongoDB
- **Email API**: Gmail API
- **Streaming**: Server-Sent Events (SSE)
